Frequently Asked Questions

Is my resume data safe?

Yes. Your uploaded files are stored securely in Firebase under your authenticated session and are never sold or used for public model training.

How is the ATS score calculated?

Our scoring algorithm grades your resume against 8 core pillars including contact formatting, section headings, achievements metrics, and keyword density.

Are there upload file format limits?

We support PDF files up to 5MB to ensure clean, structured text parsing.
